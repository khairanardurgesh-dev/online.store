## 🎨 **Design System**

### **Colors**:
- Primary: `#6366f1` (Trust blue)
- Secondary: `#10b981` (Success green)
- Accent: `#f59e0b` (Warning orange)
- Dark: `#1f2937` (Professional dark)

### **Typography**:
- Headings: `Inter` (Modern, clean)
- Body: `Inter` (Readable)
- CTA: `Inter` (Bold)

### **Components**:
- Cards: Rounded corners, shadows
- Buttons: Hover effects, transitions
- Animations: Framer Motion
- Layout: Mobile-first responsive
